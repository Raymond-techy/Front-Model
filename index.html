Knowledge for liberation

A very warm welcome to Front Model Group of Schools . We are thrilled that you found us. We use a well-structured curriculum and Prophetic Tarribiyah Development Method (PTDM) to produce a complete Muslim child. At the end of your child schooling, you will have a Refined and a Complete Muslim Child that will compete with his/her peers in the world. We are glad that you found us and have chosen to look through our website.
